About Us

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of M. H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering was inaugurated on the 29th of September, 2016 by Dr. Prasad, Faculty Advisor, NITIE Centre for Student Enterprise, and Mr. Uday Wankawala, a collaborator, facilitator, and motivator in the start-up ecosystem.Managed and supervised by Prof.Nazneen Pendhare.

Mission and Vision

The goal of MHSSCE-EDC is to inspire young minds with innovative and creative ideas. We, the MHSSCE EDC committee, aim to provide a massive platform for students to instill professional skills among themselves, while also providing opportunities for startups and motivating them to come up with innovative products and ideas for the market.
